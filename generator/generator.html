📋 Self-Contained Folder Structure & Formatting

The generated node aggregates all dropdown choices into a single comma-separated output. The node scans a subfolder named lists inside your custom node directory:

ComfyUI/ └── custom_nodes/ └── my_combination_node/ ├── your_node_file.py └── lists/ ├── hair_length.txt ├── hair_color.txt └── hairstyle.txt
  • Formatting Rule: Your text files must feature exactly one option per line.
  • Restrictions: No empty lines, no double quotes ("), and no backslashes (\). Validated on upload.
